Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does Zatsuma do?

    Its a crypto checkout for person-to-person sales, ie: a shop, market, bar, pizza delivery, girl scouts selling cookies, etc.

    Unlike a crypto wallet it can only receive funds, making it safer for use by a group of people.

  • How do I get it?

    Zatsuma has been funded by ZF Grants is available to all.

    Updates are posted on Zcash Community Forum, feel free to join in the discussion.

  • Why Zcash (ZEC) ?

    Zcash does not leak financial information.

    Good transaction times.

    Low transaction fees.

    Secure messaging (which is really cool).

    Available on many exchanges.

    Simple to install & relatively fast to setup.

    Zcash is the default coin & required by this software.

  • Why Ycash (YEC) ?

    Ycash uses the same tech as Zcash.

    Its a new project that I've helped with.

    They're nice people to work with.

  • Why Bitcoin (BTC) ?

    Everyone has heard of Bitcoin.

    Everyone who has crypto has Bitcoin.

    Available on every exchange.

    Lightning Network transactions confirm in seconds & fees are tiny.

    Bitcoin on-chain transactions are also supported.

  • How much does this cost ?

    Zatsuma is free for anyone to use.

    There are no intermediaries, funds go directly to your node wallet which only you control.

    No hosting required, it uses your computer & internet connection.

  • Cookies? Privacy Policy ?

    No personal information is collected or shared.

    Passwords are randomly generated & encrypted on the server.

    A session cookie is stored on your device when you login & encrypted on the server.

    When you logout the session cookie is deleted from your device & destroyed on the server.

  • System requirements ?

    Server :-

    • Debian/Ubuntu Linux
    • 8Gb RAM (4Gb min, but sucks!)
    • 500Gb Storage (more is better!)
    • An old laptop works well
    • Patience (downloading blockchains takes time!)

    Client (any of the following):-

    • Smartphone
    • Tablet
    • Web Browser

  • How to I send funds?

    Zatsuma can only receive funds.

    To send funds the owner of the wallet must use a node client, ie :-

    • ZECwallet
    • zcash-cli
    • bitcoin-cli
    • lncli

  • Why is BTC Lightning not always available?

    Bitcoin Lightning payments are only possible when the sale value is :-

    • Less than 0.0429 BTC (lnd daemon safety limit).
    • Less than the maximum inbound capacity of your largest active channel.

  • Why is there a dog on the front page?

    I couldn't find a picture of a canary that I liked.

  • Where do the exchange rates come from?

    Coinlib, they provide rates for 154 fiat/crypto pairs.

    To access their API you have to register.

    Coinlib has a very cool app for tracking crypto prices & portfolios.

  • How do you get a domain name for your shop?

    Dynamic DNS is provided by DuckDNS, which is a free service.

    You have to register using your Reddit, Github or Twitter account.
